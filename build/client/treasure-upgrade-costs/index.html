Note: When treasure level up fails, you gain +% increment to the base success rate, up to 10 times total.

Level Success Rate Cost Increment
1 → 2 90% 5,000 coins 🪙 +9%
2 → 3 80% 10,000 coins 🪙 +8%
3 → 4 70% 15,000 coins 🪙 +7%
4 → 5 60% 20,000 coins 🪙 +6%
5 → 6 50% 25,000 coins 🪙 +5%
6 → 7 40% 30,000 coins 🪙 + 1 Red Ticket 🎟️ +4%
7 → 8 30% 35,000 coins 🪙 + 1 Red Ticket 🎟️ +3%
8 → 9 15% 40,000 coins 🪙 + 1 Red Ticket 🎟️ +1.5%
9 → 10 5% 45,000 coins 🪙 + 1 Red Ticket 🎟️ +0.5%
10 → 11 1% 50,000 coins 🪙 + 1 Red Ticket 🎟️ +0.1%
