Actual Safe Box earnings

In What's mg/sb? we talked about what mg/sb are. If you earned no gold from enemies or challenges and only earned gold from Safe Box artifact, assuming you started with 100 gold, this is how much all the safebox levels would gain if just let that sit over 80 waves.

These are all pretty good numbers as is, but considering this is only assuming you have no other income, you can imagine how much more you'll actually get than this. With level 5 Safe Box and good gold management, you can get up to 400k gold nearly every game. With lower levels, 100k is still easily reachable, sometimes even more.
